In-depth training on UAE tax laws and corporate tax compliance.

Program Overview

The UAE Corporate Tax Course at Nitaq is designed for professionals seeking a clear and practical understanding of the UAE’s corporate tax framework. This programme equips participants with essential technical knowledge to manage corporate tax obligations, ensure regulatory compliance, and implement effective financial strategies.

Key areas of study include taxable income computation, deductions, corporate tax rates, and free zone regulations, supported by real-world case discussions. The course also explores advanced topics such as transfer pricing, tax planning, and recent corporate tax policy updates. Upon completion, participants will be well-prepared to manage tax compliance, optimize tax positions, and contribute to sustainable business growth.

Course Highlights: Master UAE Corporate Tax

  • Foundations of UAE Corporate Tax: Understand corporate tax rates, exemptions, and business registration requirements in the UAE.
  • Taxable Persons & Tax Base: Learn the distinction between resident and non-resident entities, tax residency, and permanent establishments.
