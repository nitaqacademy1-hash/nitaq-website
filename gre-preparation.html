Program Overview

The Graduate Record Examination (GRE) is a globally recognized standardized test required for admission to a wide range of graduate and postgraduate programs in the United States, Canada, and many other countries. The GRE is designed, administered, and evaluated by ETS (Educational Testing Service) and assesses a student’s readiness for advanced academic study.

The GRE Preparation Course at Nitaq is carefully structured to help students develop a strong command of Analytical Writing, Quantitative Reasoning, and Verbal Reasoning. The program focuses on concept clarity, strategic problem-solving, and exam confidence—equipping learners with everything needed to achieve their target GRE scores.

At Nitaq, our trainers are high-performing GRE achievers and experienced educators with a deep understanding of the academic and professional aspirations of students in the UAE. They recognize the unique challenges faced by international applicants and adapt their teaching approach accordingly. Our guidance goes beyond instruction—it is a mentoring partnership that supports students at every stage of their GRE journey.

Key Highlights of Our GRE Program

Nitaq offers a complete and student-focused GRE preparation experience designed to maximize performance and outcomes:

  • Unlimited Learning Resources: Students receive unrestricted access to our extensive GRE preparation library, providing all essential study materials and tools required for success.
  • Expert Quantitative & Verbal Coaching: Through the Nitaq Prep Zone, students benefit from structured instruction in GRE Math, English, and Verbal Reasoning, designed to support consistent progress throughout preparation.
  • Full-Length Practice Tests with Feedback: Realistic GRE-style practice exams combined with detailed performance analysis help students build confidence and understand real exam conditions.
  • Personalized College Consultation: Expert counseling sessions guide students through graduate program selection and help align GRE performance with long-term academic goals.
  • Dedicated Mentor Support System: Our mentor communication framework ensures direct and focused interaction between students, parents, tutors, and mentors—keeping guidance personalized and efficient.
  • 10 Full-Length GRE Mock Exams: Students experience the real GRE testing environment through ten complete mock exams that strengthen endurance, accuracy, and test-day readiness.
  • Stress-Free GRE Registration Assistance: Nitaq provides complete support with GRE registration and scheduling, ensuring students are fully prepared for exam day.
  • Section-Specific Expert Tutoring: Additional guidance is provided by specialized tutors focused on Quantitative Reasoning, Verbal Reasoning, Reading, and Analytical Writing, along with targeted score-improvement strategies.
