Program Overview

The TOEFL iBT (Internet-based Test) is a premier English-language test for university study, work, and immigration. It is accepted by more than 11,500 universities and other institutions in over 160 countries, including Australia, Canada, New Zealand, the UK, the United States, and across Europe and Asia.

At Nitaq Training Center, our TOEFL preparation program is designed to help you achieve your best score. We focus on the academic English skills needed for success in a university environment. Our expert trainers provide comprehensive coaching in all four sections—Reading, Listening, Speaking, and Writing—using authentic materials and proven strategies.

With a blend of online and offline flexibility, comprehensive study resources, and personalized mentorship, Nitaq prepares you for both the TOEFL exam and your future academic career.
