Program Overview

Planning to study or work abroad?

The TOEFL (Test of English as a Foreign Language) is a crucial step toward achieving your goals. The TOEFL Preparation Course at Nitaq Academy equips students with the strategies, techniques, and practice required to excel in all four exam sections: Reading, Listening, Speaking, and Writing.

Whether you are a first-time test taker or retaking to improve your score, our expert-led training ensures confidence and success.

What You’ll Learn

By the end of this course, participants will be able to:

  • Master strategies for each section of the TOEFL exam.

  • Gain confidence through continuous practice and feedback.
