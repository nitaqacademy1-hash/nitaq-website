Program Overview

The Certified Human Resource Manager (CHRM) program at Nitaq Training Center is designed for HR professionals and aspiring managers who want to master the strategic and operational aspects of human resources. This course covers everything from recruitment and talent management to labor laws and organizational development, providing the tools needed to lead a modern workforce effectively.

At Nitaq, we believe that people are an organization's greatest asset. Our CHRM program focuses on developing the skills required to attract, develop, and retain top talent while ensuring compliance with regional regulations like the UAE Labor Law. You will gain a deep understanding of how HR functions as a strategic partner in business success.

Through a blend of theoretical knowledge and practical case studies, our expert trainers will prepare you for the challenges of the evolving HR landscape, including digital transformation in HR and employee well-being.
